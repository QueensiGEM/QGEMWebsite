2017 Project Sponsorship

Queen's University

Queen's University is a proud sponsor of QGEM, providing us with funding, lab space, lab equipment, and the ability to reach out to students as an official QGEM club as part of the Arts and Science Undergraduate Society (ASUS). In return, we are proud to be an educational-based club for the entire student body to learn about the fundamentals and applications of synthetic biology. In addition, we would also like to thank the Engineering Society of Queen's University (EngSoc) for helping us host the QGEM website.
